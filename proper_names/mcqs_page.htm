Rules

  • Duration of the event is 90 minutes
  • The main questions of the event will be based on aptitude and logic.
  • There are 4 main questions each with 4 subparts
  • The points allotted to each question and each subpart will be based on the difficulty level.
  • For each main question, one needs to attempt all its subparts for successful submission.
  • Only one submission is allowed per question.
  • There is no negative marking.
  • The distractions would pop up randomly for a short duration and will carry some bonus points if completed within that duration.
  • Participants are allowed to close the distraction window if they wish too.
  • Bonus points will be provided based on the performance with the time of distraction pops up, even if the task is not completed within that duration.
  • Individual Participation.
  • No prerequisite knowledge is required.
  • Any form of malpractice would lead to disqualification.
  • Authorities decision will be final and binding.
